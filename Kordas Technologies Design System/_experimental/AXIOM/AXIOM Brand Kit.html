// 07

Voice & Tone

Principles

Precise, not verbose.

Say the minimum needed. Engineers respect brevity. No marketing padding.

Confident, not arrogant.

State facts. Show results. Don't oversell — the work speaks.

Technical, not exclusive.

Use precise terms. But explain context — not every reader is a ML engineer.

Human, not corporate.

Real people wrote this. Use contractions. Occasional dry humour is fine.

Examples

DO

"Deploy to 94 nodes in under 15 minutes."

DON'T

"Our cutting-edge solution empowers your organisation to leverage synergies."

DO

"From brief to working prototype in 4 weeks. Ugly but real — you can touch it."

DON'T

"We deliver world-class rapid prototyping services tailored to your needs."
